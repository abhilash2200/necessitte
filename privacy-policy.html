Privacy Policy – Necessitte Aesthetic Studio

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

At Necessitte Aesthetic Studio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website www.necessitee.in or use our services.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this page regularly to stay informed about any updates. Your continued use of the Site after any modifications will be deemed as your acknowledgment and acceptance of the updated policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personally identifiable information when you:

  • Book an appointment
  • Sign up for newsletters or promotions
  • Fill out a contact form or feedback survey
  • Participate in contests or giveaways
  • Contact us via phone, email, or the website

This may include your name, email address, phone number, postal address, service preferences, and any details you voluntarily provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Send appointment reminders or follow-ups
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our services and personalize your experience
  • Send promotional emails or offers (only if you opt-in)
  • Manage contests or special events
  • Conduct internal business analysis and research

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who agree to keep your data confidential.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Security

We take appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial measures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. They help us analyze site traffic, understand user behavior, and improve functionality. You can accept or decline cookies via your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if disabled.

Your Consent

By using this website, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
