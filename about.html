About Us – Necessitee Aesthetics

Welcome to Necessitee Aesthetics—where confidence meets beauty, and every skin journey finds its radiance.

Conveniently located in the heart of Gurugram, our clinic is more than just a skincare destination; it’s a space where your personalised path to healthy, luminous skin begins.

At Necessitee, we are committed to building an environment where you feel visible, supported and at home because your skincare journey deserves professionalism and a sense of belonging.

Our Story: Selfcare as a Necessity

Necessitee was founded on a belief that self-care as a choice could be a necessity, between our co-founders, Teeya Karki and Vinay. Our belief that everyone should feel secure and at ease in their own skin has been at the core of our brand for more than five years.  Our goal is to provide you with the best possible cosmetic treatment experience, and we work hard to make it both affordable and efficient. Necessitee is a combination of innovation, luxury and care. The primary aim is to provide each client with meaningful experiences that will change their lives.

We understand how emotionally taxing skincare challenges can be—acne, patchy tone, or signs of ageing. In our Gurugram clinic, we can help you realise your self-esteem again. We do it through technology and loving care. From FDA-approved devices to the latest in clinical skincare innovations, we have it all. We are here to help you rebuild your self-confidence, one session at a time.

Introducing Teeya Karki

Clinical Cosmetologist and Founder

Teeya Karki is the driving force behind Necessitee's revolutionary skincare philosophy.  She contributes a wealth of knowledge influenced by her work with well-known multinational companies and her in-depth knowledge of changing skincare trends and technology, as well as more than ten years of experience in the beauty and aesthetics sector.

Teeya's methodology is based on results-driven care, in which the journey of every client is meticulously, empathetically, and creatively designed.  She views skincare as a means of achieving vibrant, self-assured, and true self-expression rather than just a cure.  Her dedication and enthusiasm continue to influence the core of each and every Necessitee experience.

Teeya ensures a customised and outcome-driven experience by taking the time to fully understand each client's skin concerns while maintaining a warm and attentive demeanour.  Using the most cutting-edge laser and clinical technology or conventional skincare techniques, she creates personalised treatment plans that produce quantifiable results.  Teeya's dedication to perfection keeps her at the forefront of the field, always updating her knowledge to include the best methods and high-end items.  Her only objective, together with the team at Necessitee, is to make sure each client departs feeling strong, self-assured, and at ease in their own skin.

GET TO KNOW VINAY KARKI

Co-Founder & Visionary

Our co-founder, Vinay, offers Necessitee a potent combination of technical know-how and business vision.  He guarantees that every facet of our operations, from user-friendly online reservations to data-driven growth initiatives, operates with accuracy and efficiency thanks to his solid background in business research and software development.

Vinay has a strong interest in fusing technology with wellness, creating a smooth and customised client experience.  His goal is to create a brand that is adaptable to the ever-changing aesthetics business while also being scalable and sustainable.

More than merely outcomes, Vinay wants to establish a clinic environment that changes both Skin and Soul, where creativity and sincere care coexist, and each patient is treated with respect.

Why Necessitee?

Skincare is a very personal experience, and we at Necessitee recognise that.  Our method starts with listening, comprehending, and helping you make genuine, long-lasting adjustments, regardless of whether you are dealing with hyperpigmentation, acne scars, or just wanting to improve your natural shine.

Our clinic, which is situated in the centre of Gurugram, combines FDA-approved, clinically proven technologies with an opulent, tranquil setting that exudes cosiness, warmth, and elegance.  You are immediately made to feel at home in an environment that is both professional and intimate.

Our focus is on your individual skincare journey.  At every stage of your treatment, our team is dedicated to your comfort and self-assurance. We will assess the skin and only recommend the best, effective, tailored treatments to meet your personal objectives. We are more than treatment providers—we are partners in your skin journey. At Necessitee, every visit is an opportunity to feel seen, heard, and genuinely cared for. We promise to provide solutions that are customised to meet your specific needs so you can rediscover the satisfaction of having glowing, healthy skin and the confidence that goes along with it.

Our Promise to You

We guarantee not only outward results but inner skin resilience, enhanced function, and ageless radiance — combining cutting-edge medical science with the sensual indulgence of authentic self-care.
